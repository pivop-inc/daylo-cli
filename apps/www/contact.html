§ 03Security Reports

Send suspected vulnerabilities, leaked-key concerns, provider-token issues, or account-access issues to hello@pivop.jp. Include request IDs, timestamps, affected account email, and reproduction steps when available.

§ 04Legal and Data Requests

For account deletion, access, correction, suspension of use, privacy, or terms questions, contact hello@pivop.jp. The controlling legal pages are the Japanese Privacy Policy and Terms; English convenience translations are also available.
